Help & Guide

How to use the system

Products: Add and manage items sold in the shop.

Stock: View stock levels and delete discontinued items.

Sales: Record product sales and auto-update stock.

Reports: View total sales, stock, and charts.

Common Questions

Q: Is data saved online?
A: No, data is stored locally using browser storage.

Q: Can deleted products be recovered?
A: No, deleted products are permanently removed.
